Experience Design / Visual Design / Interaction Design / Development
Web Application
Power your education empire.
Turn Up was designed as an extension of the freelancing platform Free-Lancer.eu, with Turn Up being a space for freelancers to gain and share knowledge, via the platform they are most used to.
Designed for both you and your audience.
Turn Up was designed having in mind both the lecturer and the listener, the goal was to help people create sustainable, lasting, effective articles and courses, that make in impact. Each peace and tool was built with the audience's learning and sharing experience in mind.
Business success takes a team - welcome to yours.
From having operated in the field for years, Freelancer knows the ins and outs of creating successful projects and even businesses, and they want to show that they are there for you to inspire, motivate, coach and celebrate with you.
