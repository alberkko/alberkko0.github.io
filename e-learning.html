Faculty of Architecture and Urbanism in Tirana,
E-Learning Platform.
Mobile App Design / Interaction Design / Development
Web Application
Online learning made simple.
An online learning platform, allowing students of the University of Architecture and Urbanism in Tirana, to tak online classes, examination and monitor their progress. As well as allowing teachers to manage their classes, teach and examine students online.
An efficient way to manage and assess progress while enhancing connections.
By allowing students to submit their work online, teachers can keep track of their sections, view grades easily, and mark assignments during any free time they have, without having to carry stacks of paper around.

This solution would also prove valuable for the faculty during COVID, as their departments were ready to continue teaching and adapting to the new study-from-home rules.
Class management has never been easier.
Student management in one screen. For each of their classes, teachers can manage their students and monitor their progress. The platform also offers the ability to generate reports and statistics for each class and general department statistics for department directors and administration staff.
Remotely administer any kind of tests online.
Using the platform teachers can create multiple types of tests, quizzes and courses. The platform is designed to give control over the entire testing process and ability to test and monitor.
