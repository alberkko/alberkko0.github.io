An every young community.
Hyperfluids started up in 2013 with intention of dressing young people who are engaged with their environment, who live in the community and relate to each other. Working with Hyperfluids was an incredible experience, watching them expand their brand with my help.
Textile collection backed up by the footwear, accessory, jewellery, fragrance, and sunglasses lines.
With Hyperfluids brings the latest international trends to the street in the form of easy, comfortable and casual clothes. The Hyperfluids collections are conceived with the idea of dressing men and women with a young mindset, taking into account that age is not an obstacle when choosing our wardrobe. The challenge was in finding a way to convey that messaging through the branding.
